Envelope Templates

Printing Express does not provide envelope templates, as the placement of artwork on envelopes is regulated by the US Postal Service. If you need envelope templates, many sizes are available on the USPS website ( Win/ Mac). PDF's of standard envelopes (#10) are available on the USPS website as well as #9 Return Envelopes. Please note these templates are provided by the United States Postal Service and Printing Express does not guarantee the usability of these templates.

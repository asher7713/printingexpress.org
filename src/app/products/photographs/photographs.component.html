Gallery Wraps

Gallery wraps are canvas prints that don't require a frame. The image is wrapped around bars which form a "frame", but on the inside of the print. When viewed from the front, top, left, right, or bottom, all you see is canvas photo. These have become wildly popular due to the unique "gallery" look, and it's an excellent value compared to traditional framing and matting.

Museum Wraps

Museum wraps are canvas prints that are traditionally framed. If you'd like a more traditional photograph look for your canvas photo, this is the option for you. Printing Express can do the printing, the wrapping, the framing, and the matting to make your memories look exceptional.
